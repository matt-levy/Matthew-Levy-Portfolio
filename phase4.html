Phase 4

About the Project

Phase 4 is a 4v4 online multiplayer brawler. Play as one of four ferocious elementals to battle for control over timed objectives. Work with your team to capture points and defeat your enemies.

View the project here.

My Role

In this project, I was mainly responsible for programming movement and all things required for the game loop. I designed the 'capture the objectives' gamemode, programmed respawning and spectaing, and all movement related abilites. I also was responsible for programming environmental interactions, such as our gas geysers and electric fences.

I also contributed to this game's netcode, combat system, and particle effects.
